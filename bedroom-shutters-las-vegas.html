Frequently Asked Questions

Are plantation shutters good for bedrooms?

Plantation shutters are excellent for bedrooms. When fully closed, they block nearly all light for better sleep. They also provide superior insulation to keep bedrooms cool in summer and warm in winter, plus complete privacy from neighbors.

Do shutters block light better than blinds?

Yes, plantation shutters block significantly more light than blinds. The solid louvers and frame create a tighter seal against the window, eliminating the light gaps common with blinds. For maximum light blocking, we recommend 3.5-inch louvers.

Can bedroom shutters help with Las Vegas heat?

Absolutely. Plantation shutters provide excellent insulation, keeping your bedroom cooler in the brutal Las Vegas summers. The solid construction creates an air barrier that helps maintain comfortable sleeping temperatures while reducing AC costs.

How much do bedroom shutters cost in Las Vegas?

Bedroom shutters typically cost $250-$600 per window depending on size. Larger bedroom windows cost more, but the investment pays off in better sleep and energy savings. We offer free in-home quotes and will beat any competitor's price by 10%.
