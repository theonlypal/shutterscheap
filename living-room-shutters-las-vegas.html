Frequently Asked Questions

Are plantation shutters good for living rooms?

Plantation shutters are ideal for living rooms. They provide elegant light control, protect furniture from UV damage, reduce glare on TVs, and add timeless architectural beauty. They're especially valuable in Las Vegas where intense sunlight and heat make quality window treatments essential.

Do shutters help with TV glare in living rooms?

Yes! Plantation shutters are excellent for controlling TV glare. Simply tilt the louvers to redirect sunlight away from your screen while still allowing natural light into the room. This is something blinds and curtains can't do as precisely.

How do shutters handle large living room windows?

We custom-build shutters for any window size, including large picture windows and sliding glass doors common in Las Vegas living rooms. Multi-panel configurations allow you to open just the sections you need for access or ventilation.

How much do living room shutters cost in Las Vegas?

Living room shutters typically cost $300-$800+ per window depending on size and configuration. Large picture windows and sliding doors cost more but have the biggest visual impact. We offer free in-home quotes and will beat any competitor's price by 10%.
