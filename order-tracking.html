Your order timeline

Every order follows a clear process from start to finish. Here's what to expect after placing your order:

  • Order Confirmation: You'll receive confirmation within 24 hours of placing your order, including a detailed summary of your selections.
  • Manufacturing: Custom shutters and blinds are precision-made to your exact specifications. Manufacturing typically takes 2-4 weeks depending on product type.
  • Quality Check: Every product is inspected before shipping to ensure it meets our quality standards.
  • Shipping Notification: You'll be notified when your order ships from the manufacturer to our facility.
  • Installation Scheduling: Once your order arrives, we'll contact you to schedule a convenient installation date.
  • Professional Installation: Our experienced installers complete the job efficiently and leave your home clean.
