Our Selection

Shades for Every Room & Style

Roller Shades

Clean, modern lines with versatile light control. Roller shades offer a sleek, minimalist look that works beautifully in any room. Available in light-filtering or blackout fabrics.

  • Light-filtering & blackout options
  • Hundreds of colors & patterns
  • Motorization available
  • Cordless child-safe operation

Zebra Shades (Dual Shades)

The best of both worlds - alternating sheer and solid fabric bands let you shift between privacy and natural light. Raise the shade completely or adjust the bands for perfect light control.

  • Alternating sheer & solid bands
  • Adjustable light control
  • Modern, stylish design
  • Day & night versatility

Cellular Shades (Honeycomb)

Energy-efficient honeycomb construction traps air to insulate against Las Vegas heat. Single, double, or triple cell options provide varying levels of insulation and light control.

  • Superior energy efficiency
  • Reduces heating & cooling costs
  • Sound dampening properties
  • Top-down/bottom-up options

Roman Shades

Timeless elegance with soft fabric folds. Roman shades add warmth and sophistication to any space, available in flat, hobbled, or relaxed styles to match your decor.

  • Classic, elegant design
  • Multiple fold styles
  • Hundreds of fabric choices
  • Blackout lining available

Woven Wood Shades

Natural beauty from bamboo, jute, and grasses. Woven wood shades bring organic texture and warmth to your windows, perfect for adding natural elements to your design.

  • Natural bamboo & grass materials
  • Unique texture & warmth
  • Eco-friendly options
  • Privacy liner available

Sheer Shades

Soft, diffused light with a view. Sheer shades combine the softness of fabric with adjustable vanes that tilt for light control while maintaining your outdoor view.

  • Soft, diffused natural light
  • Tilting vanes for light control
  • Maintains outdoor view
  • UV protection
