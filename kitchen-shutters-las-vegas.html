Frequently Asked Questions

Are plantation shutters good for kitchens?

Yes, plantation shutters are excellent for kitchens. Poly vinyl shutters are moisture-resistant, easy to wipe clean from cooking grease, and won't warp from heat or humidity. They offer precise light control for task lighting and add significant home value.

What type of shutters are best for kitchen windows?

Poly vinyl (composite) shutters are the best choice for kitchen windows. They resist moisture from cooking steam, are easy to clean with a damp cloth, won't absorb cooking odors, and handle temperature fluctuations. We recommend avoiding real wood shutters in kitchens due to moisture concerns.

How do you clean kitchen shutters?

Kitchen shutters are easy to clean. Simply wipe with a damp cloth or use mild soap and water. For grease buildup near the stove, a mild degreaser works well. Poly vinyl shutters won't absorb grease or odors like fabric window treatments do.
