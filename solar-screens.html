Why Solar Screens?

Protect Your Home from Desert Heat

Heat & Energy Savings

Solar screens absorb 72%-84% of the sun's heat before it reaches your glass. This keeps your home cooler, reduces air conditioning strain, and lowers your energy bills significantly.

  • Blocks up to 84% of heat transfer
  • Reduces A/C workload
  • Lower monthly energy bills
  • ROI in 2-2.5 years

Glare & UV Protection

Eliminate 100% of glare on screens and reduce eye strain. Solar screens also protect your furniture, flooring, and artwork from fading caused by harmful UV rays.

  • 100% glare elimination
  • UV protection for furnishings
  • Reduces eye strain
  • Preserves interior colors

Turf & Landscape Protection

Low-E windows can reflect intense heat that melts artificial turf. Solar screens prevent this by absorbing the heat before it can damage your landscaping.

  • Prevents turf melting
  • Protects landscaping investment
  • Stops window reflection damage
  • Extends turf lifespan

Privacy & View

Enjoy daytime privacy - you can see out clearly while people outside cannot see in. Maintain your view of the outdoors while adding a layer of security to your home.

  • Daytime privacy
  • See out, they can't see in
  • Maintains outdoor view
  • Added security layer

Sound Reduction

Solar screens reduce sound transmission at the glass by up to 52%. Enjoy a quieter home environment, especially beneficial for homes near busy streets.

  • 52% sound reduction
  • Quieter home environment
  • Great for busy streets
  • Enhanced comfort

Multiple color options available to satisfy any HOA requirements. Our screens are designed to blend seamlessly with your home's exterior aesthetic.

  • Multiple color options
  • HOA palette compatible
  • Professional appearance
  • Custom color matching
