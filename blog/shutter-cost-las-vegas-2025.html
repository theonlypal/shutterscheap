Poly Vinyl vs. Wood Shutters: Which is Better for Las Vegas?

This is the most common question we get, and in Las Vegas, the answer is usually poly vinyl (composite) shutters. Here's why:

Poly Vinyl Shutters: Best for Desert Climate

  • Heat resistant: Won't warp or crack in 115°F+ summer temperatures
  • Moisture proof: Perfect for bathrooms, kitchens, and pool-adjacent rooms
  • UV stable: Won't yellow or fade in intense desert sun
  • Energy efficient: Blocks up to 73% of heat transfer through windows
  • 30-40% cheaper than equivalent wood shutters
  • Longer warranty: Most come with 25-year coverage

Real Wood Shutters: When They Make Sense

  • Historic homes where authentic materials matter
  • Interior rooms without direct sun exposure
  • Homeowners who prefer natural wood grain appearance
  • Custom stain matching for existing woodwork
Pro Tip: If you love the look of wood but want desert durability, ask about composite shutters with a wood-grain finish. They look nearly identical to real wood but handle Las Vegas heat much better.

What Affects Plantation Shutter Pricing?

Several factors determine what you'll pay for shutters in Las Vegas:

1. Window Size and Shape

Standard rectangular windows are the most affordable. Specialty shapes cost more:

  • Arched tops: Add 25-40% to base price
  • Circle/octagon: Add 40-60%
  • Extra-large windows: May require reinforced frames
  • French doors: Typically priced per panel

2. Louver Size

Louvers (the horizontal slats) come in different widths:

  • 2.5" louvers: Traditional look, most affordable
  • 3.5" louvers: Most popular, great light control
  • 4.5" louvers: Modern aesthetic, premium price

3. Frame Style

The frame around your shutters affects both look and cost:

  • L-frame: Most economical, simple installation
  • Z-frame: Covers more window trim, mid-price
  • Deco frame: Decorative molding, premium price

4. Installation Type

  • Inside mount: Fits within window frame (most common)
  • Outside mount: Covers entire window opening (for shallow windows)

How to Save Money on Shutters in Las Vegas

1. Order Multiple Windows at Once

Most companies (including us) offer better per-window pricing on larger orders. Ordering 10+ windows often includes free professional installation - a savings of $500-$1,500.

2. Choose Poly Vinyl Over Wood

You'll save 30-40% with no sacrifice in durability. In fact, poly vinyl typically outlasts wood in our desert climate.

3. Go Local Instead of Big Box

Home Depot, Lowe's, and national chains typically charge 40-60% more than local specialists. They're also using subcontractors for installation.

4. Get Multiple Quotes

Always get at least 3 quotes. Make sure you're comparing apples to apples - same materials, similar warranties, and professional installation.

5. Ask About Price Matching

Reputable local companies will match or beat comparable quotes. At Shutters Cheap, we offer a Lowest Price Guarantee - bring us any written quote and we'll beat it.

Timing Tip: Fall and winter (October-February) tend to be slower seasons for shutter companies. You may find better deals and faster installation times during these months.

Red Flags When Shopping for Shutters

Watch out for these warning signs:

  • No in-home measurement: Accurate sizing requires measuring your actual windows
  • Pressure tactics: "This price is only good today" = run away
  • Vague warranties: Get specifics in writing (parts, labor, fading)
  • No local presence: Out-of-state companies can be hard to reach for warranty service
  • Unusually low prices: May indicate thin materials or poor construction
