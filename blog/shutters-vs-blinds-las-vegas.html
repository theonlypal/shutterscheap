Shutters vs Blinds: Which is Better for Las Vegas Homes?

A detailed comparison to help you choose the right window treatment for the desert climate.

Updated December 2025 | By Shutters Cheap

Quick Answer:

Shutters are better for most Las Vegas homes. They last 3-5x longer, block more heat, increase home value, and are easier to clean. Blinds cost less upfront but need replacement more often.

Head-to-Head Comparison

Feature Plantation Shutters Faux Wood Blinds Winner
Cost per sq ft $20-35 $8-15 Blinds
Lifespan 25+ years 5-10 years Shutters

Cost Comparison for a Typical Las Vegas Home

Let's compare the true cost of shutters vs blinds over 25 years for a typical Las Vegas home with 10 standard windows (approximately 200 sq ft of window coverage):

Shutters (25-year cost)

  • Initial cost: $4,000-$7,000
  • Replacements needed: 0
  • Repair costs: ~$0
  • Total: $4,000-$7,000

Blinds (25-year cost)

  • Initial cost: $1,600-$3,000
  • Replacements (2-4x): $3,200-$12,000
  • Repair costs: ~$200-$500
  • Total: $5,000-$15,500

When to Choose Shutters

Shutters are the better choice when:

  • You plan to stay 5+ years - ROI improves the longer you own them
  • West-facing windows - Need maximum heat blocking
  • Selling soon - Shutters increase home value; blinds don't
  • HOA communities - Always approved, premium appearance
  • Hate cleaning - Much easier to maintain
  • Large windows - Shutters handle large spans better

When to Choose Blinds

Blinds might be better when:

  • Tight budget - Need to cover many windows affordably
  • Rental property - Lower upfront investment
  • Short-term home - Moving within 2-3 years
  • Small windows - Shutters may look oversized on very small windows
  • Secondary rooms - Garages, utility rooms, workshops

Desert-Specific Considerations

Las Vegas Climate Factors:

  • Intense UV - Shutters offer better UV protection
  • Dust - Shutters are much easier to clean in dusty conditions
  • Dry air - Wood blinds can crack; faux wood or poly vinyl recommended

Our Recommendation

For most Las Vegas homeowners, plantation shutters are the better long-term investment. While blinds cost less upfront, the combination of durability, energy savings, home value increase, and easier maintenance makes shutters the smarter choice for primary living spaces.

That said, we install both shutters and blinds. If budget is a concern, consider a hybrid approach: shutters for main living areas and bedrooms, blinds for secondary spaces.
