Polywood vs Wood Shutters: Which is Best for Las Vegas?

A detailed comparison from 20+ years of installing shutters in the Las Vegas desert.

Updated December 2024 | By Shutters Cheap

Quick Answer:

Polywood (poly vinyl/composite) shutters are the better choice for 90% of Las Vegas homes. They handle extreme heat better, cost less, and require zero maintenance. Choose wood only if natural grain texture is essential to your design vision.

The Las Vegas Climate Challenge

Las Vegas presents unique challenges for window treatments:

  • 115째F+ summer heat - Can warp and crack certain materials
  • Intense UV exposure - 300+ sunny days per year causes fading
  • Low humidity - Causes wood to dry and split over time
  • Dust - Requires easy-to-clean surfaces

After installing thousands of shutters across the valley, we've seen what works - and what doesn't.

When to Choose Polywood

Choose polywood shutters if:

  • You have west-facing windows that get intense afternoon sun
  • You want shutters in bathrooms or kitchens (moisture resistance)
  • You prefer low maintenance - no refinishing ever
  • You want maximum energy efficiency (73% heat blocking)
  • You want to save 30-40% vs wood shutters
  • You want white or off-white shutters (most popular in Vegas)

When to Choose Wood (Basswood)

Choose wood shutters if:

  • You want a custom stain color to match existing woodwork
  • Natural wood grain texture is essential to your design
  • Windows are not in direct intense sunlight
  • You're willing to maintain and refinish periodically
  • You're installing in a luxury home where budget isn't primary

Our Recommendation

After 20+ years of installing shutters in Las Vegas homes, we recommend polywood for most customers. Here's why:

  1. Performance - Polywood handles Vegas heat better and lasts longer without maintenance
  2. Value - You get a premium look at 30-40% less cost
  3. Energy savings - Higher heat blocking means lower AC bills
  4. Bathroom-safe - Can use the same shutters throughout your home

That said, we install plenty of basswood shutters for customers who want custom stains or natural wood aesthetics. Both are quality products backed by our 25-year warranty.
