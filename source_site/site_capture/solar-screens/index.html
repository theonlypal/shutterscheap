What Will Solar Screens DO for You?

Solar screens will absorb 72% to 84% of the heat. This keeps cold air in longer during the summer and warm air in longer during the winter.

They will stop 100% of the glare.

Reduces decibel level of sound at the glass by 52%.

Eliminates bleaching, fading and dry rot of your carpets, furniture and drapes.

Eliminates 92% of dust, dirt and crud by creating a seal around the existing window frame.

Increases privacy because you have a clear view out but people can’t see inside during the day.

Eliminates melting of artificial turf from intense glare off of windows.
