Beautiful Shutters, at a Affordable Cost

Shutters are a beautiful addition to every home or business. They help control privacy, lighting, heat and sound insulation all while keeping windows and doors accessible for everyday use. Pre-framed shutters for standard size windows are fast and easy to install. However, large windows, sliding glass doors and custom shape windows are also available in shutters.

The Las Vegas heat is no match for our shutters which are constructed with poly vinyl. Poly Vinyl is more durable and longer lasting than real wood because it is scratch resistant, water resistant, fade resistant and does not require oiling or other exhausting maintenance. Best of all, poly vinyl is more affordable than real wood and it looks the same!

Forget traveling all around town just to see sample products! We bring the showroom to you with our free in home estimate. We will measure your windows, show you our sample products and answer any questions you may have. Call us today to schedule your free estimate.

Lower Your Power Bill

Shutters stop 73% of the heat and qualify for the Energy Tax Credit. With shutters closed, the insulated factor of your windows is significantly increased. This helps reduce noise, maintain temperature and lower energy costs. Save money and energy today, with shutters!

Reinforced with Metal

Our Poly-Aluminum shutters are reinforced from within with extruded aluminum for extra strength and rigidity. This premium feature is essential for long lasting support and comes standard in all our Poly-Aluminum shutters.  

Sand blasted shutters also available

Our Basswood Shutters can be sand blasted! With sand blasted shutters you get the look and feel of real wood. This unique upgrade will add another level of sophistication and detail to your shutters. Ask us for more information.
