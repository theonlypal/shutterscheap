            About Us

             

            Shutters Cheap is a local Las Vegas business with over 40 years of experience in providing and installing new shutters, blinds and solar screens. We are your reliable and friendly window covering professional for both Residential and Commercial projects. From plantation shutters to vertical blinds – we have it all! Our quotes are always free, include a free measuring of all your windows and a free gift. Call us today and we can answer any questions you might have about our products, the installation process or our pricing.

            Save time and let us come to you! Instead of traveling store to store to view products, we bring our mobile showroom directly to you. Select your future window treatments in the comfort of your own home. This way you can be certain the product you choose will work perfectly with your existing home décor and style. Call us today and we can schedule a time that works best for you.

            When you purchase new shutters, blinds or solar screens you are investing in the privacy, energy efficiency and comfort of your home. Shutters add value to your property and make every room look and feel more elegant. Blinds are a fast and affordable way to increase privacy while still maintaining a stylish appeal. Solar screens block up to 90% of UV rays which reduces the heat coming into your home and helps lower your power bills. If you are not sure which product is right for you, we can help you decide.

            We are certified dealers for: Century and Grebar, Norman, Designer Fabrics, Sunland Shutters, Onyx Premium Shutters, Farrway Shutters, Elegant Shutters, US Polymor Shutters and San Benito Shutters.
