Frequently Asked Questions About Las Vegas Shutters

What window treatments work best in Las Vegas’s extreme heat?

Poly-vinyl shutters and cellular blinds perform exceptionally well in Las Vegas’s extreme temperatures. Poly-vinyl resists warping, expanding, and fading even in temperatures exceeding 115°F, while cellular blinds provide superior insulation that can reduce cooling costs by 15-20% annually.

How much can window treatments reduce my Las Vegas energy bills?

Quality window treatments typically reduce Las Vegas cooling costs by 12-20% annually through superior insulation and heat reduction. Solar screens can block up to 90% of heat gain while maintaining views, and plantation shutters provide excellent temperature control year-round.

Do you service all areas of the Las Vegas Valley?

Yes! Our mobile showroom serves all Las Vegas Valley areas including Paradise, Whitney, Winchester, Spring Valley, Providence, Nellis AFB, and Sunrise Manor, plus surrounding communities. We bring our complete selection directly to your home regardless of location.

What’s the difference between basswood and poly-vinyl shutters for Las Vegas homes?

Basswood offers natural beauty with custom staining options but requires occasional maintenance. Poly-vinyl provides maintenance-free performance with superior resistance to Vegas heat, humidity, and UV exposure. Both options include our comprehensive warranties and are excellent choices depending on your preferences and budget.

Most Las Vegas installations are completed in 2-4 hours depending on the number of windows and complexity. Our efficient process minimizes disruption to your daily routine while ensuring perfect results. We’ll provide an accurate timeline during your free consultation.
