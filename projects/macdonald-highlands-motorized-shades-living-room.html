Motorized Roller Shades — MacDonald Highlands Living Room

A floor-to-ceiling living room with 14-foot wall of glass overlooking the Strip from a Dragonridge custom home. The homeowner wanted view-preserving solar control with one-touch operation tied to their smart-home.

Install photo coming soon

The challenge

Glare on the TV, heat gain from the south-facing exposure, and a desire to preserve the iconic Strip view at all times. Manual operation was not practical at that height.

What we installed

Six motorized roller shades in 5% openness solar fabric, dropping from a single recessed pocket above the windows. Wired to a Lutron hub so the homeowner can program 'morning,' 'afternoon glare,' and 'movie night' scenes via their existing smart-home system.

The outcome

The view stays visible behind the shades at all times. Glare is gone. The HVAC unit cycles less often on south-facing days. The homeowner can trigger all six shades from voice control or their phone.

Project specs

  • 5% openness solar fabric
  • Lutron motorized operation
  • Hidden ceiling pocket
  • Smart-home compatible
  • Charcoal fabric for view preservation
  • Lifetime motor warranty
