Whole-Home Blind Replacement — Spring Valley

A 14-window Spring Valley home with original 20-year-old vinyl builder-grade blinds — yellowed, brittle, several with missing slats and tangled cords.

Install photo coming soon

The challenge

Budget-conscious replacement that still looked premium. Five different window sizes to coordinate. Some windows had pets that had chewed the original blinds.

What we installed

2-inch faux wood blinds in white across all 14 windows. Faux wood looks like real wood but resists humidity and is much more durable against pets. Cordless lift on the lower bedrooms for child safety.

The outcome

Whole-home replacement under budget. House looks 'new again' per the homeowner. Faux wood is wipe-clean and resists the dent-and-warp issues their original vinyl had.

Project specs

  • 2" faux wood blinds
  • Bright white finish
  • 14 windows total
  • Cordless lift in bedrooms
  • Inside-mount
  • Same-day removal of old blinds
