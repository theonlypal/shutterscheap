Plantation Shutters — Summerlin Master Bedroom

A 4-window master bedroom suite in The Trails, Summerlin. The homeowner wanted café-style operation so the top half could open for morning light while the bottom half stays closed for privacy from the neighboring lot.

Install photo coming soon

The challenge

West-facing windows took direct afternoon sun. The original 2-inch faux wood blinds were warped and warming the bedroom several degrees above the rest of the house.

What we installed

We installed 3.5-inch louver polywood plantation shutters in pure white, frame-mounted for a built-in look. The wider louver gives a cleaner sightline with fewer horizontal lines, and the polywood blocks roughly 73% of heat transfer compared to bare glass.

The outcome

The room is now 6-8°F cooler in the afternoon. The homeowner can leave the upper halves angled open for daylight while keeping the lower halves closed for privacy. Total install was about 4 hours.

Project specs

  • 3.5" louver polywood
  • Pure white finish
  • Frame-mounted in existing window casings
  • Hidden tilt rod
  • 25-year warranty
  • HOA-approved for The Trails
