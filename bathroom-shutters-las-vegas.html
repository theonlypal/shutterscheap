Why Shutters Beat Other Bathroom Options

vs. Fabric Blinds

Fabric blinds absorb moisture and can develop mold. Shutters are completely waterproof and wipe clean.

vs. Frosted Glass

Frosted glass is permanent with no light control. Shutters let you choose: full privacy, partial light, or wide open.

vs. Window Film

Film provides no adjustability. Shutters let you control exactly how much light enters while maintaining privacy.

vs. Curtains

Curtains trap moisture, develop odors, and need frequent washing. Shutters stay fresh with a quick wipe.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can you put plantation shutters in a bathroom?

Yes! Poly vinyl (composite) shutters are perfect for bathrooms. They're 100% waterproof, won't warp from steam or humidity, resist mold and mildew, and provide the privacy and light control bathrooms need. We recommend poly vinyl over wood for all bathroom applications.

Are plantation shutters good for bathrooms with showers?

Yes, poly vinyl plantation shutters are excellent near showers. They're completely waterproof and won't be damaged by steam, humidity, or water splashes. Unlike wood shutters that can warp or fabric treatments that can mold, poly vinyl stays perfect in wet environments.

Do bathroom shutters provide privacy?

Yes, bathroom shutters provide excellent privacy. When closed, they block all views from outside. The adjustable louvers let you control exactly how much light enters while maintaining complete privacy—something you can't do with most other window treatments.

How much do bathroom shutters cost in Las Vegas?

Bathroom shutters typically cost $200-$450 per window depending on size. Smaller bathroom windows are often among the most affordable shutter installations. We offer free in-home quotes and will beat any competitor's price by 10%.
